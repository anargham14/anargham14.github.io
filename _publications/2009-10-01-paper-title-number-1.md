This paper is the culmination of the work I started during my short term project at the National Astronomical Observatory of Japan (NAOJ), Mitaka, Tokyo, under the supervision of Dr. Maria Dainotti. I was involved with the project from May, 2024. My involvement extended well into the next year. 

Cosmological model-building is obviously one of the challenges that attraced me towards the subject in the first place. The greatest tests of such an endeavour, are the so-called 'cosmological tensions'. Whether hinting at new physics or unaccounted systematic biases, these test the current models to the extreme, and are fertile grounds for discoveries. This was the set-up that led to my involvement in the project.
