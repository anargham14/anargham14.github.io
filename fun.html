Interests

I am interested in large-scale structure formation in the universe. Specifically, I am interested in how massive neutrinos affect LSS, and how this can be studied to answer fundamental questions about these particles. I enjoy solving research problems with a computational and theoretical flavour, but also appreciate insights from observational data.

Constraining the sum of neutrino mass eigenstates with k NN CDFs

I am using k Nearest Neighbour Cumulative Distribution Functions to investigate whether cross-correlations between the total matter field and dark matter halo positions can be used to constrain cosmological parameters with Fisher information.

Investigating the Hubble Tension with combined Type Ia SNe data

We examined the Hubble tension using the Supernovae Ia data from the Pantheon, Pantheon+, Joint Lightcurve Analysis, and Dark Energy Survey catalogs combined together into the so-called Master Sample. This resulted in a publication in the Journal of High Energy Astrophysics.
