Constraining the sum of neutrino mass eigenstates with k NN CDFs

The rationale behind this page is to serialize the developments of this project and help me keep the bigger picture in mind. I do not intend it to be a blog for this project, but would be quite useful to get an idea of what we aim to do.

Motivation

Massive neutrinos have a significant impact on large-scale structure formation, as has been studied in many works. It is worthwile to try and cosntrain the sum of the mass of the three flavor eigenstates using N-body simulations, which has also been investigated at length. What hasn't been done is trying to quantify how much information about the parameter $M_nu=\displaystyle\sum m_\nu$ can be obtained from k-nearest neighbour distribution functions. That is what we investigate here.
