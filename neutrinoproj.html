Constraining the sum of neutrino mass eigenstates with k NN CDFs

The rationale behind this page is to serialize the developments of this project and help me keep the bigger picture in mind. This is supposed to be a semi-professional blog for this project, that lists the ingredients that went into building this project from scratch.

Motivation

Massive neutrinos have a significant impact on large-scale structure formation, as has been studied in many works. It is worthwile to try and cosntrain the sum of the mass of the three flavor eigenstates using N-body simulations, which has also been investigated at length. What hasn't been done is trying to quantify how much information about $M_{\nu}=\sum m_{\nu}$ can be obtained from k-nearest neighbour distribution functions. That is what we investigate here.

Massive Neutrinos and their Effect on Large-Scale Structure Formation

Massive neutrino cosmologies have been studied at length in several reviews, the most prominent amongst them being the review by Lesgourges and Pastor (2006). I plan to upload my notes based on this review, that I have prepared while working on this project, in the near future.
